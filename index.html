Welcome!

Art has been a major hobby of mine since middle school when I used IbisPaint to make digital art on my phone. These days, I use an [XPen] tablet and FireAlpaca to create digital art although I also use IbisPaint from time to time.

I started learning to crochet when I was 8. My dad had a friend who ran a yarn shop, and we went there to take her crochet lessons that she also sold. In recent years, I've gotten into Amigurumi (phonetics/pronunciation guide here) or the art of knitting or crocheting dolls or figures. The practice formally started in Japan; hence, why English borrows the Japanese word for it.
